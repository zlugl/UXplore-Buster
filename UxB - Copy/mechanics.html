GAME MECHANICS

How to Play UxPlore Buster

📋 Game Flow

The game is divided into 5 progressive levels. Each level must be completed in sequence before unlocking the next. Follow these steps:

1. Select a Level → 2. Read the Lecture/Tutorial → 3. Answer the Quiz → 4. Check Score → 5. Unlock Next Level

📊 Scoring System

Your performance is measured through a point-based system:

Total Score: 500 Points

Each level quiz is worth 100 points

Per Question: 10-25 Points

Varies by question difficulty

Passing Score: 70% or Higher

You must achieve at least 70% to pass a level and unlock the next one

🎯 Level Structure

Each level contains:

📖 Lecture

Read comprehensive learning material covering the level's topic

❓ Conceptual Quiz

Answer multiple-choice questions to test your understanding

🎨 Practical Challenge

Apply your knowledge to design or coding problems

📈 Result & Feedback

Get detailed feedback on your answers and improvement suggestions

❓ Question Types

You'll encounter different types of questions throughout the game:

Multiple Choice

Select the best answer from 4 options

Design Scenario

Choose the correct design element or principle for a given situation

Code Challenge

Write or debug HTML/CSS code (Levels 4-5)

True or False

Determine if a statement about UX/UI design is correct

🔓 Progression System

Levels are locked until you complete the previous level:

Level 1

Available

Level 2

Locked

Level 3

Locked

Level 4

Locked

Level 5

Locked

Complete Level 1 with a score of 70% or higher to unlock Level 2, and so on.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • 📚 Read the entire lecture before attempting the quiz
  • 🤔 Take time to understand each question before answering
  • 📝 Keep notes of important concepts you learn
  • 🔄 Review your scores to identify weak areas
  • 💪 You can retake a level to improve your score
  • 🎯 Focus on understanding, not just memorizing
